SWEek of Welcome

Welcome to Cornell Engineering and the Cornell SWE family! Below is a schedule of the 2019 SWEek of Welcome! Events are open to all Cornell students!

Saturday, 8/31
Welcome Picnic, tie-dye, outdoor games, and photo booth 12:00pm-2:00pm on the Pew Engineering Quad

Tuesday, 9/3
September GBody @ 4:30 pm in Phillips 101
Join us for our first general body meeting of the semester to learn about SWE, meet our leaders, and learn how to get involved

Wednesday, 9/4
SWEesters in STEM Panel @ 4:30-5:30 pm in Phillips 403
Come listen to female professors and graduate students discuss their experiences and give tips for conquering the new school year.

Wednesday, 9/4
Cookies, Cards, and Cornell @ 7-9pm in Tatkon Center (North Campus)
Decorate cookies, write cards for patients at Upstate Golisano Children's Hospital, and pick the brains of SWE upperclassmen.

Thursday, 9/5
DiverSWEty in Engineering Photo Campaign @ 10am-2pm in Duffield (Workday Table)
Stop by to take a picture and show your support for diversity in technical fields.

Thursday, 9/5
SWE Summer Book Club @ 4:30pm in Hollister 314
Come informally discuss the SWE summer book club read, The Only Woman in the Room.

Friday, 9/6
SWE x AOE Scavenger Hunt @ 4:30pm (meet on Engineering Quad)
Engage in a fun scavenger hunt around the Engineering Quad to celebrate the end of the first week of classes!

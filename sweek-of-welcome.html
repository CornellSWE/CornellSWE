SWEek of Welcome

Welcome to Cornell Engineering and the Cornell SWE family! Below is a schedule of the 2020 SWEek of Welcome! Events are open to all Cornell students!

All events will be held via Zoom and start at 4:30 p.m. EDT!
Link for all events: https://cornell.zoom.us/j/91577119932?pwd=cWNSYlVKZmZtN0M4NXJ6T1FFMTlZUT09

Sunday, 9/6
Cards and Cornell
Join us for an afternoon where we learn cool origami skills while talking about our experiences so far going to college during a worldwide pandemic. Come and meet fellow SWesters and pick up a new skill too!

Monday, 9/7
Speed Friendling
Come and join us for this event to meet fellow female engineers in SWE. This will be a speed networking event where we will answer interesting questions and get to learn more about one another. Come and make new friends in SWE!

Tuesday, 9/8
SWE General Body Meeting
Come and meet the new members of our E-board and listen to an information session by Sensata Technologies Inc.

Wednesday, 9/9
Online Game Night
Come to our Game Night for a fun Night playing virtual games with other SWesters. We are playing Scribble, Codewords and Jackbox games!

Thursday, 9/10
Escape Room
Do you love Harry Potter? Come and put your knowledge to the test at this event where we will complete a Harry Potter themed virtual escape room!

Friday, 9/11
Netflix Party
We will be watching The Kissing Booth 2 together! So if you loved the first movie, or just wanna re-watch the second with us, come!

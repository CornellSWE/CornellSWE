Graduate Student Programs

This branch was created during the fall semester of 2010. Even though we are young, we have a lot of great ideas and are continuing to grow. Come to one of our events to see what we are all about!

GOAL: Provide support to women pursuing graduate degrees in engineering through social, networking, outreach, and professional development events.

  • See upcoming events and updates on the Cornell Graduate SWE facebook page!
  • Our Cornell Graduate SWE blog provides information about how to get involved, information about our upcoming events, and more!

Cornell Graduate SWE Mailing List:

To sign up for the Cornell Graduate SWE mailing list, please input your Cornell e-mail address (netID@cornell.edu) in the email field. You will be automatically subscribed to the gradswe-l mailing list.
